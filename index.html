Going Offline

  • Determine what data you need and expose it
  • Create an Offline Data Downloader
  • Store your data in client side persistence (localStorage)
  • Store a value indicating where data should be pulled from

Cache Manifest

  • Add your manifest='/cache.manifest' attribute to the html element.
  • Determine your assets by seeing what your page loads.
  • Create your cache.manifest file with the required assets in the CACHE section.
